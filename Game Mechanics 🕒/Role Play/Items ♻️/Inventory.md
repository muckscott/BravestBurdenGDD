The **Backpack** is the inventory system. It currently has *5* pockets:
1. [[Consumable Items]]
2. [[Weapons]]
3. [[Headpiece]]
4. [[Chest-piece]]
5. [[Footwear]]

Each pocket has *18* slots for items. Some items can be stacked, the max stacked amount is defined on a per item basis.
