Ranged weapons revolve around the timing or preparation of the shot.
A well timed shot will have an added benefit depending on the weapon type.
	**Shortbow**: headshot (CRIT)
	**Longbow**: pierces
	**Crossbow**: knockback
	**Spear**: bleeds & slows
